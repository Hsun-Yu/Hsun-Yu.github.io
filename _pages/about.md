I am an M.Sc. student in Computer Science at [EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch/en/), specialising in computer science theory. My research interests include data-centric AI, reinforcement learning, robustness, extrapolation, and causality. More broadly, I am interested in how diversity can help shape better AI systems and a better society.

I am currently a project student at the [MLO Lab](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/mlo/) at EPFL, where I work with [Martin Jaggi](https://people.epfl.ch/martin.jaggi?lang=en) and [El Mahdi Chayti](https://people.epfl.ch/el-mahdi.chayti?lang=en). Previously, I interned with the [Robust Machine Learning Group](https://is.mpg.de/rml) at the [Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems](https://is.mpg.de/) and the [CKIP Lab](https://ckip.iis.sinica.edu.tw/) at [Academia Sinica](https://www.sinica.edu.tw/en).

Feel free to reach out if you would like to discuss research or potential collaboration.
